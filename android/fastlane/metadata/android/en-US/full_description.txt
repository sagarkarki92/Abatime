Aba time is a free movie app where you can browse and download the movie you like to watch.
You can surf the movie app completely free without any registration.
Major feature of this movie app are :
	- View movies list according to genre.
	- Search desired movie
	- Add movies to watch list
	- Watch trailer of any movie for free 
	- View movies classified as per downloads, favourites, stats.
Available genres:
      - Action
      - Thriller
      - Crime
      - Romance
      - Adventure
      - Drama
      - Sci-fi
      - Animation
      - Fantasy
      -Mystery
All movies are free to browse, free to download in any quality(720p, 1080p)
